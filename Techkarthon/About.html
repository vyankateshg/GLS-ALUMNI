🎓 Welcome to GLS Alumni Network

Connecting past, present, and future students for mentorship, career opportunities, and lifelong learning.

Who We Are

The GLS Alumni Network is a dedicated platform that brings together graduates, mentors, and students to foster professional and personal growth. Whether you're looking to **connect with fellow alumni, seek mentorship, or participate in career-building events**, this platform is built for you.

What We Offer

  • 🤝 Alumni Networking - Connect with your batchmates and industry professionals.
  • 📚 Mentorship Programs - Learn from experienced alumni in your field.
  • 🚀 Career Opportunities - Find job postings and internships.
  • 🎉 Events & Workshops - Participate in hackathons, conferences, and training sessions.

How It Works

  • Alumni can register, update profiles, and connect with students.
  • Students can seek guidance, explore jobs, and attend events.
  • Admins verify users, moderate content, and track engagement.

Strengthening University Legacy

  • Alumni contribute to the university's growth by sharing success stories, funding scholarships, and mentoring students.
  • Creates a lasting bond between past and present students, fostering a culture of giving back.
